The Zaytoona Story

"Food is not just nourishment for the body, but also for the soul."

Founded in 2010, Zaytoona Restaurant began as a dream to bring the authentic flavors of the Middle East to our community. Our founder, Chef Ahmed Hassan, grew up in Lebanon, where he learned the art of traditional Middle Eastern cooking from his grandmother.

Every recipe at Zaytoona has been passed down through generations, carefully preserved and perfected to maintain the authentic taste and aroma that makes Middle Eastern cuisine so special.

Our name "Zaytoona" means "olive" in Arabic, symbolizing peace, prosperity, and the Mediterranean way of life. Just like the olive tree that gives us its precious fruit, we strive to provide nourishment and joy to everyone who visits our restaurant.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Ahmed Hassan

With over 20 years of culinary experience, Chef Ahmed brings his passion for authentic Middle Eastern cuisine to every dish he creates. Born and raised in Beirut, Lebanon, he learned the secrets of traditional cooking from his grandmother, who taught him the importance of using fresh, quality ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Chef Ahmed's philosophy is simple: great food comes from great ingredients, prepared with love and respect for tradition. He personally oversees every dish that leaves our kitchen, ensuring that each bite tells a story of heritage and authenticity.
