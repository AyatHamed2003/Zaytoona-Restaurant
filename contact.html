Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of all sizes. You can make a reservation by calling us at (555) 123-4567 or by filling out our contact form.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We offer catering services for events of all sizes. Our catering menu includes both traditional Middle Eastern dishes and custom options. Contact us for more details.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we have a wide variety of vegetarian and vegan options. Many of our traditional dishes are naturally vegetarian, and we're happy to accommodate dietary restrictions.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have a dedicated parking lot for our customers. Street parking is also available in the surrounding area.
